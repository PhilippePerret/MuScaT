Use this script to "open" something in MuScaT, starting with the
analysis folder or the manual.

  #{'./utils/open.rb manual'.jaune}

USAGE
-----
  #{'./utils/open.rb ["]<what>["]'.jaune}

  (avec l'alias `mus`)  > mus open [<what>]

WHAT
----

  "What" can be :

  #{'manual'.jaune}

      To open the MuScaT manual in english.

  #{'<analysis name>'.jaune}

      To open the analysis with name "<analysis name>".
